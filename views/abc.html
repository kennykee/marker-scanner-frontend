Conservation Effects in Singapore
Active, Beautiful and Clean
(ABC) Waters Programme

The soft- scaping of our waterways has an important role to play in benefiting our biodiversity. Soft edges, with a variety of native plants, help to merge the man-made and the natural environments together. Planted edges create more habitat types, and more places for animals to seek shelter, rest and feed.

From 2010 to 2021, 90 projects have received the ABC Waters Certification through PUB's sustained efforts in encouraging adoption of the ABC Waters Concept by both public and private sectors.
