Conservation Effects in Singapore
The Eco-link @ BKE

An ecological bridge that spans the Bukit Timah Expressway, connecting Bukit Timah Nature Reserve and Central Catchment Nature Reserve. Its main purpose is to restore the ecological connection between two nature reserves, allowing wildlife to expand their habitat, genetic pool and survival chances.

The 62m-long wildlife link is the first purpose-built bridge for wildlife in South-east Asia. It can be seen by motorists on the Bukit Timah Expressway (BKE) about 600m north of Rifle Range Road.
